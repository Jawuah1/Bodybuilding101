Bench Press

"Bench press is a popular strength training exercise that primarily targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps muscles. It is performed by lying on a flat bench with a weighted barbell held over the chest, then pushing the barbell upwards until the arms are fully extended, and lowering it back down to the chest."

Barbell Squat

"Squats are a fundamental strength training exercise that target the lower body muscles, including the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. The exercise is performed by standing with feet shoulder-width apart, holding a weighted barbell on the back of the shoulders, and bending the knees and hips to lower the body until the thighs are parallel to the ground, and then standing back up."
